Welcome to MonGuessr

MonGuessr is a variant of GeoGuessr with Monash University (currently Clayton only). You will be presented with a picture of the campus, and must find it on a map, the closer your guess to the real location the more points you score.


This site is still in alpha, please report bugs if you find them
